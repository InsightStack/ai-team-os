# Business reviews

Periodic reviews of how the business is doing. Weekly, monthly, or quarterly depending on your team's rhythm.

## Doc index

- `{{YYYY-Www}}.md`: weekly reviews. ISO week format, e.g., `2026-W19.md`.
- `{{YYYY-MM}}.md`: monthly reviews.
- `{{YYYY-Qn}}.md`: quarterly reviews.

## What goes in a weekly review

A useful weekly review is short and decision-focused. A working structure:

1. **Headline.** One sentence: are we winning, losing, or unclear this week?
2. **Movers.** What metric moved the most and why.
3. **Decisions made.** What the team decided this week, with one-line context.
4. **Open questions.** What the team is debating, with a date for resolution.
5. **Next week.** What you're trying to land.

Keep it under one printed page. If you can't fit it on one page, you're confusing reporting with thinking.
